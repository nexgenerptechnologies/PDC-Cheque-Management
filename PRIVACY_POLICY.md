# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-31

Nexgen ERP Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the PDC Cheque Management App ("the App").

### 1. Information We Collect
The App is designed to run entirely within your self-hosted or managed ERPNext / Frappe instance. We do not independently collect, store, or transmit your personal data, customer data, banking information, or transaction history to any external servers or third parties. All data remains completely under the ownership and control of your ERPNext administrator.

### 2. How Your Information is Used
Because all data resides locally on your Frappe server, your information is used solely by your organization to automate banking, process journal entries, and manage cheque clearances. 

### 3. Third-Party Access
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any of your data to outside parties. The App does not integrate with any external telemetry or tracking systems.

### 4. Data Security
The security of your data depends on the security configurations of your Frappe framework and server environment. We recommend keeping your ERPNext installation updated and following industry-standard security practices.

### 5. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the repository.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
